Remote controlled ball launcher

This is a design for a semiautomatic ball launcher that can be attached to a remotely controlled car I had previously developed.

The rack and pinion mechanism with missing teeth, typical of airsoft gearboxes, retracts the platform and compresses the springs. When the last teeth is reached, the compression is released and the ball propelled. The microswitch stops the motor after a revolution is completed.
